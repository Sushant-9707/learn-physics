    Work, Energy & Power

    Work and energy provide an alternative way to analyze motion. Instead of tracking forces and acceleration, energy methods focus on the ability to do work and how it transforms.

    Core Ideas

    Work

    Work is done when a force causes displacement of an object. It depends on the magnitude of the force, the displacement, and the angle between them.

    No displacement means no work, even if a force is applied.

    Kinetic Energy

    Kinetic energy is the energy possessed by an object due to its motion. It depends on the mass of the object and the square of its velocity.

    Faster motion dramatically increases kinetic energy.

    Potential Energy

    Potential energy is energy stored due to position or configuration. Gravitational potential energy depends on height in a gravitational field.

    It represents the ability to do work in the future.

    Work–Energy Theorem

    The work done by all forces acting on an object equals the change in its kinetic energy.

    This theorem provides a powerful alternative to Newton’s laws.

    Power

    Power is the rate at which work is done or energy is transferred. It measures how quickly work can be performed.

    High power means rapid energy transfer.
