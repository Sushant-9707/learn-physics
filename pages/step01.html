STEP 01

Units, Measurements & Dimensional Analysis

Physics begins with measurement. Without a system of units and dimensions, equations lose meaning and comparisons become impossible.

Core Ideas

SI Units

The International System of Units (SI) defines standard units used globally for consistency in measurement.

Fundamental & Derived Units

Fundamental units form the base of measurement. Derived units are combinations of these fundamental units.

Dimensional Analysis

Dimensional analysis checks the correctness of equations and helps derive relationships between physical quantities.

Errors & Significant Figures

Every measurement has uncertainty. Understanding errors helps improve accuracy and reliability.
